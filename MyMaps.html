Camping in Ontario Parks

Growing up in Ontario was a dream. My family made a concious effort to teach my siblings and I about outdoor activites such as camping, hiking, canoeing etc. I have been camping more times than I could possibly recall. I find Ontario Parks to be the easiest method for camping. There are parks all over Ontario. My favourite time of year to go camping is fall. Fall means no bugs, sunshine and empty parks :) I have also had the opportunity of working for Ontario Parks at Six Mile Lake. The summers I spent there were unforgettable. Parks employees are passionate and excited about their work! But I mean, how could you not be?!

Below is a map displaying the Ontario Parks I have visited in the past few years. I generally utilize parks for camping but, I also find myself in parks for other activites. My favourtie park would have to be Killbear Provincial Park, because of the precious memories I have made there.

As much as I love walking...nothing beats being on the water! Nowadays I love to canoe from the various access points to discover Lakes and portages new to me!
